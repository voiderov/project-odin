Ramen Recipe

A comforting bowl of homemade ramen with a savory broth, springy noodles, and simple toppings. This easy version focuses on building flavor quickly while staying approachable for weeknight cooking.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 packs fresh or dried ramen noodles
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon mirin or rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch fresh ginger, sliced
  • 2 soft-boiled eggs (optional)
  • Sliced scallions, nori, and sliced pork or tofu for topping

Steps

  1. In a pot, combine broth, water, garlic, and ginger. Bring to a gentle simmer for 10â€“15 minutes to develop flavor.
  2. Strain out solids, then return the broth to the pot. Stir in soy sauce, mirin, and sesame oil. Keep warm.
  3. Cook ramen noodles according to package instructions, drain, and divide into bowls.
  4. Pour hot broth over noodles and top with soft-boiled eggs, sliced scallions, nori, and your protein of choice.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy while hot.
